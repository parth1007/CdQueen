About Us...

CodeQueen is an annual coding event conducted by Aparoksha.
Hosting teams of codesses from all across the nation, CodeQueen provides an esteemed platform for women to exhibit their mettle in competitive coding and to encourage them to pursue this craft.
CodeQueen is thus, for apt women aspiring to grow and improve through perseverence and skill. .
